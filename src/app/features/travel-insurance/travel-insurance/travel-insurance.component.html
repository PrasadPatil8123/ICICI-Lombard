What’s covered under ICICI Lombard's travel insurance?

Hospitalisation expenses for illness and injury

A medical emergency during your trip could mentally and financially drain you. Our travel insurance policy covers hospitalisation expenses during your travel.

Personal liability

Legal expenses arising from bodily injuries or property damage to a third-party could be taxing. Our policy helps cover the legal costs associated with such mishaps.

Lost, delayed, or damaged baggage

Our travel insurance policy covers you for expenses incurred due to loss of baggage, delay (more than six hours) of baggage and even damage of baggage in transit.

Emergency cash

If you need emergency cash during your trip because of a lost wallet, we will help you arrange it.
