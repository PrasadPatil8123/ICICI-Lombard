What is Cashless Hospitalisation?

Cashless hospitalisation means that you do not have to pay any cash or have a financial transaction with the hospital directly. It enables you and your family can focus on treatment and recovery without worrying about the bills piling up. Within Cashless Mediclaim all your medical expenses are borne by your insurance provider.

How Does Cashless Hospitalisation Work

When availing cashless hospitalisation, be sure to check the network hospital list before getting admitted. This list contains the names of all the hospitals that your insurer has tied-up with to provide cashless Mediclaim.

Note:ICICI Lombard reserves the right to add or remove any Hospital from the given list. Cashless facility may be limited to specific room category or Services available in some hospitals. Please check the same with hospital before admission.
